Who are you, anyway?

Almost forgot to say hello. I'm Fatima Rabi, currently finishing the Advertising and Marketing Communications Management program at Algonquin College.

Most of my process happens before I open any design software. Research the audience, understand the competition, figure out what actually needs to be said. Then make it hard to ignore.

When I'm not solving communication challenges, I have fun with personal projects that scratch the creative itch that I can't seem to get rid of.

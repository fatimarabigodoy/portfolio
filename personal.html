Made in Peru

Digital Illustration

Turns out moving away from your home country at 18 gives you a lot to unpack, creatively speaking. This illustration combines traditional Peruvian imagery with a contemporary illustration style, far more clean than mine. Sometimes the constraint of anchor points makes you try something different.

Created using Adobe Illustrator.

Breasts and Eggs Cover Redesign

Summer break reading led to highlighting every other page of this book. When a book moves you that much, you have to do something with that energy, right? Unsolicited cover redesign for Mieko Kawakami's novel. Focuses on symbolic imagery, and creating shelf appeal.

Slightly more abstract than my usual work, but sometimes you follow where the concept leads.

A Cat Named Molly

Sequential Art

Nothing is stronger than the bond between a girl and some cat that doesn't even know she exists. Originally shared as an Instagram carousel, this 8-slide story explores how small habits—spotting a neighbourhood cat during coffee runs with my parents—can become unexpectedly significant when you're navigating distance and change.

Made in Procreate.
